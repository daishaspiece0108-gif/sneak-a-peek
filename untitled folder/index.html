Meet Ashley J. Rose, RDMS OB/GYN BR

Welcome to Sneek-A-Peek Ultrasound Boutique — Fredericksburg's first and only elective ultrasound boutique owned and operated by Registered Diagnostic Medical Sonographers (RDMS).

Owner Ashley J. Rose is a Board Certified, OB/GYN registered ultrasound technologist with over 15 years of experience performing diagnostic medical OB/GYN ultrasounds in the Fredericksburg area.

Every exam is performed ONLY by ARDMS-registered sonographers specializing in Women's OB/GYN services. Your safety and your family's safety will always be our top priority.

Office Policy

Please review our policy prior to your scheduled appointment.

🏥 Health & Safety

  • All guests must be in good health
  • Please reschedule if you or any guest have flu-like symptoms or COVID exposure
  • Maximum 6 people total (including mom)
  • Children count as guests
  • No switching guests in and out during the session

📋 Check-In Procedure

  • Send us a message via FB Messenger or text when you arrive
  • Additional info will be on the front door
  • Please do not call the office multiple times to check in
  • Wait for our message indicating the office is clean and ready
  • We sanitize all equipment and surfaces between guests

🔄 Cancellations & Returns

  • Please provide 24 hours notice when cancelling
  • No Call/No Show fee will be charged
  • Free return visit if no images obtained due to baby's positioning
  • Free return must be scheduled within 14 days of initial visit
  • Return visits, First Peek & Later Peek sessions are NOT eligible for free returns

Important Disclaimer

Sneek-A-Peek Ultrasound Boutique is an ELECTIVE ultrasound imaging facility. All clients must establish prenatal care with an OB physician and have a diagnostic ultrasound completed prior to visiting. We do NOT perform diagnostic ultrasounds. If we have any concerns regarding your health or your baby's health, your OB/GYN will be notified promptly.
